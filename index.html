FAQ

Simple answers before you start.

Is Sobrio a medical app?

No. Sobrio is designed for personal tracking and support. It is not a substitute for professional medical, emergency, or clinical care.

Do I need a subscription?

Yes. Users need an active subscription to use Sobrio and access the app's features.

Can I track savings?

Yes. You can add your estimated daily spending and Sobrio will turn it into simple savings insights over time.
